32nd Hanoi Open Chess
Tournament 2020

The 32nd Hanoi Open Chess Tournament 2020 brings together the best chess players worldwide of all time to fight in single matches in quick games and where the final winner will fight for the crown against the current world champion Magnus Carlsen. This tournament is held every two years in a different country, and this year we bring to Hanoi the show and the passion for chess. For two intense days we can enjoy the highest level games between champions of different times and with different styles. So if you are passionate about this sport you can not miss the biggest event of the year.

32nd Hanoi Open Chess Tournament 2020 Partners

In order to carry out this event, many companies have contacted us to help with their organization and promotion.
Among them we find large companies from different sectors but all with immense passion for chess.
Thanks to them and to our president Arkady Dvorkovich this event could be held.
